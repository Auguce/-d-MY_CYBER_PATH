Key Project: Key Exchange using Neural Networks

Honours Year Computer Science Project, University of Liverpool, UK
(Sept 2022 - Jun 2023)

Under the guidance of Professor Alexei Lisitsa, I conducted academic research on the Kanter-Kinzel-Kanter (K-K-K) protocol. The project involved the development of a Python prototype to analyze the protocol's performance and investigate its robustness against genetic algorithm-based attacks.
